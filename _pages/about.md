My name is Joanna Radoła, I'm a PhD candidate working on code-switching (CS) in language models - examining LLMs' code-switching abilities, CS detection and language identification, improving how CS is handled in Machine Translation and in pre-training corpora collection pipelines... My research is supervised by [François Yvon](https://fyvo.github.io/) at [ISIR](https://www.isir.upmc.fr/), an institute affiliated with [Sorbonne University](https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/), and by [Josep Maria Crego](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=lw_aQqQAAAAJ&hl=en) at [SYSTRAN](https://www.systransoft.com/).
